Getting Here

  • 🚶
    On foot: From Patan Durbar Square, walk south along the galli lanes towards Walkhu. We're about 10 minutes walking.
  • 🛺
    By tempo/taxi: Tell your driver "Walkhu Galli, Lalitpur." Most local drivers know the area. A short ride from the city center.
  • 🚗
    Parking: Street parking available nearby. We recommend arriving by foot or two-wheeler if possible.
  • 📍
    Landmark: Look for the green signboard with the reclining lady. You'll know it when you see it.
