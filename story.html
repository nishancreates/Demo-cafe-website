The Detox Club began not as a business, but as a belief — that a place of true nourishment, quiet, and community could exist in the heart of Lalitpur.

The Origin

How it began

In 2026, a simple vision took root in Walkhu Galli — a café that would serve not just food and drink, but a feeling. A feeling of being truly present, nourished, and unhurried.

Every item on our menu was crafted with intention. Every corner of our space was designed to invite stillness. The Detox Club is not just where you eat — it's where you arrive.

We believe that what you consume shapes how you feel, and how you feel shapes who you become. So we begin each day asking: what does our community need today?

The Space

Where you arrive

A café that holds you the way a good book does — warmly, without hurry.

Tucked into Walkhu Galli, The Detox Club is designed to feel like an exhale. Soft light, warm tones, and the gentle sound of something brewing — always.

We have corners for solo contemplation, tables for quiet meetings, and an open energy for those who just want to feel less alone in the city.

The aesthetic is organic — warm parchment, sage greens, the kind of textures that remind you to slow down. Every detail was chosen to make you feel welcome.

Come with your journal. Come with your team. Come as you are. There is always a place for you here.
